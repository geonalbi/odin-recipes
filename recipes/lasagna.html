Lasagna

This is the generational secret recipe that has been passed down to me by my grandma...

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles(duh)
  • Minced beef
  • Feta cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions & garlic for the flavour
  • Love(loads of it)

Time to cook(step by step)

  • Boil the noodles but not too much!!
  • Cook the beef but not too much!!!
  • Blend the tomatoes...
  • Cut down the onions and the garlic!
  • Throw the carlic, onions and tomatoes into a bowl and mix em up!
  • Layer the lasagna and put a tin foil on top and bake
  • Let them rest before serving
  • Once served, shred some feta and pour some love

Enjoy!!!
